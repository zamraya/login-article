KADASIG GYM

STRENGTH AND SPIRIT IN MOTION



The Kadasig Gym at CTU Danao serves as a dynamic center for sports, fitness, and community events. Equipped with facilities for basketball, volleyball, and other sports, it provides students with a space to engage in athletic activities, stay active, and build teamwork skills. Whether it's a practice session or an inter-school competition, the Kadasig Gym supports students' physical health and competitive spirit, adding excitement to campus life.



Beyond athletics, the Kadasig Gym also hosts a variety of events, from school assemblies to cultural performances, making it a versatile space for gatherings. Its spacious layout and seating capacity allow it to accommodate large audiences, providing a welcoming environment for community celebrations and ceremonies. This adaptability makes the Kadasig Gym a vital part of campus, bringing students, faculty, and guests together for shared experiences.



The gym's role extends beyond physical activities, as it also fosters a sense of camaraderie and school pride among students. It's a place where friendships are formed, and achievements are celebrated, strengthening the bond within the CTU Danao community. The Kadasig Gym, with its multifunctional use and vibrant atmosphere, truly embodies the spirit of unity and school pride at CTU Danao.
