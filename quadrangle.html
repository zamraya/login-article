THE QUADRANGLE

A SQUARE OF GATHERINGS



The CTU Danao Quadrangle serves as a central, open gathering space that plays a significant role in campus life. Located at the heart of the university, the Quadrangle is a versatile venue for various events, from flag ceremonies and campus assemblies to cultural festivals and performances. Its spacious design provides ample room for large gatherings, making it an ideal spot for events that bring together students, faculty, and staff, fostering a strong sense of community and school spirit.



Aside from official events, the Quadrangle is also a popular spot for students to relax, study, and socialize. With its open-air setting and easy accessibility, it offers a refreshing break from indoor study spaces, giving students a place to unwind between classes. The Quadrangle’s welcoming environment encourages casual interactions and friendly conversations, enhancing the campus experience by creating a place for students to connect.



The flexibility and central location of the CTU Danao Quadrangle make it an essential part of campus life. It serves not only as a gathering space but as a symbol of unity for the CTU Danao community, where students come together to celebrate, learn, and grow. The Quadrangle is more than just an open area—it's a focal point of campus tradition and student life.
